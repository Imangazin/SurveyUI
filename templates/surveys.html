Survey Management

Instructions:
  1. Click Add Survey to create a new survey.
  2. Enter the survey Name, Description, Start Date, and End Date, then click Save.
  3. To modify an existing survey, click the Edit button in the Actions column.
  4. After a survey is created, click Upload to upload a CSV file containing student survey links.
Brightspace update schedule: Changes made in Survey Managament area are not reflected in Brightspace immediately. Any survey or survey assignment created or updated here will appear in Brightspace only after the next 7:00 AM run.
